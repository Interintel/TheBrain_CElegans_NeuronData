PVPL

Function: Along with PVQL, PVPR pioneers the left side ventral cord and this function appears to be a joint action of these two processes. When PVPR precursor is ablated in the embryo, PVQL and AVKR growth cones migrate towards the right side. (Durbin, 1987; Antebi et al., 1997).
