AWAR

Function: Chemotaxis to diacetyl, pyrazine, trimethylthiazole (Bargmann et al., 1993). One of the three core sensory neurons (AWA, AWC, ASK) that are required for sexual-attraction in males (White and Jorgensen, 2012).
