AVL

Function: Defecation. AVL and DVB are excitatory GABAergic motor neurons for the enteric muscles (Avery and Thomas, 1997; McIntire et al., 1993). Together with DVB, activates expulsion muscle contraction (E.p or EMC), also required for anterior body contraction (aBoc) step in defecation motor program (DMP).
