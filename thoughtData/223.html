RMGR

Function:
- Functions as the center of a hub-and-spoke circuit, which works bidirectionally due to gap junctions, for pheromone attraction and social behavior. RMG is suggested to integrate signals from various sensory neurons, and, in turn, set the activity state for these neurons, modulating their chemosensory responses (Macosco et al., 2009; Lockery, 2009).
